Turkey Chili

Description

Healthy turkey chili, not as good as regular chili, but its healthy and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey
  • Jalepeno
  • Red Pepper
  • Pinto Beans
  • Celeray
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chicken Stock
  • Bay Leaf
  • Cumin
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Canned Tomatoes

Directions

  1. Cook beans with onions and garlic
  2. Brown turkey
  3. Cut up veggies
  4. Brown veggies in same pot as turkey
  5. Add chicken stock, canned tomatoes, salt, pepper, and herbs
  6. Add in beans
  7. Simmer on low for 2-3 hours
  8. Serve
