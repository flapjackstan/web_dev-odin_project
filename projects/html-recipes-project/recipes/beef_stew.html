Beef Stew

Description

Stew with beef and veggies. Surprisingly very tender!

Ingredients

  • Ribeye
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Unflavored Gelatin
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chicken Stock
  • Rosemary

Directions

  1. Cut up ribeye to small chunks
  2. Brown ribeye chunks
  3. Cut up veggies
  4. Brown veggies in same pot
  5. Add chicken stock
  6. Add salt, pepper, herbs, and gelatin
  7. Simmer on low for 2-3 hours
  8. Serve
